for the DevSecOps team behind Tecton's managed platform.

- Grew a one-person function to eight direct reports, owning platform operations,
  infrastructure product work, business IT, and compliance & security.
- Ran daily operations across all of Tecton's deployment models, scaling to 100+
  single-tenant customer-hosted, Tecton-managed, environments in addition to
  larger enterprise deployments.
- Partnered with product, engineering leadership, and customers to build the
  infrastructure enterprise adoption required.
- Led architecture direction, hiring, mentorship, and incident response.
